SOC09108 - 2015 - Practical 04

Extending WordPress: themes

This week, you have to finish the past weeks practical and ask questions.

Then, create a fully customised theme by reading the developer documentation and the WordPress Codex.

The aim of this practical session is not for you to develop a fully customised theme, but have an overview of the required work, what strategies you can use to avoid some of the work (use a scaffolding theme), and compare it with your previous experience with child themes.
